# How to Find Contact Information for Prospects

This guide shows you exactly how to find names and emails for all 50 prospects in your target list.

## Method 1: LinkedIn (FREE - Best for Names)

### Step-by-Step:

1. **Go to LinkedIn** (linkedin.com - free account works)

2. **Use the search bar** at the top with this format:
   ```
   "Job Title" Company Name Location
   ```

3. **Example for Cologix:**
   ```
   "VP Operations" Cologix Toronto
   ```
   OR
   ```
   "Facilities Director" Cologix Toronto
   ```

4. **Filter results:**
   - Click "People" tab
   - Under "Location" â†’ Select "Greater Toronto Area"
   - Under "Current company" â†’ Type the company name

5. **Find the right person:**
   - Look for titles like:
     - VP Operations
     - Director of Facilities
     - Facilities Manager
     - Energy Manager (rare but perfect!)
     - Plant Manager (for manufacturing)

6. **What to do when you find them:**
   - **Option A:** Send LinkedIn message directly (free 50/day limit)
   - **Option B:** Note their full name and use Method 2 to find email

## Method 2: Email Finding (FREE Tools)

### Tool 1: Hunter.io (FREE - 25 searches/month)

1. Go to **hunter.io**
2. Enter the **company domain** (e.g., cologix.com)
3. Hunter shows you:
   - Email format (e.g., firstname.lastname@cologix.com)
   - Sample verified emails
   - Pattern confidence %

4. **Apply the pattern:**
   - If pattern is `firstname.lastname@company.com`
   - And you found "John Smith" on LinkedIn
   - Email is: `john.smith@company.com`

### Tool 2: Email Format Guessing (100% FREE)

**Most common corporate email formats:**
1. `firstname.lastname@company.com` (60% of companies)
2. `firstname@company.com` (20%)
3. `firstinitiallastname@company.com` (10%)
4. `firstname.l@company.com` (5%)

**How to verify without sending:**
1. Go to **mail-tester.com/email-verifier** (free)
2. Enter your guessed email
3. It tells you if the email exists (without sending)

### Tool 3: Apollo.io (FREE - 100 contacts/month)

1. Sign up at **apollo.io** (free account)
2. Search by:
   - Company: "Cologix"
   - Location: "Toronto, Ontario"
   - Title: "VP Operations" OR "Facilities Director"
3. Apollo shows:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Phone number (sometimes)
   - LinkedIn profile link

**This is the easiest method - use Apollo first!**

## Method 5: Cold Calling to Get Contact Info (FREE)

**This works surprisingly well:**

1. **Call the main switchboard** (numbers provided in CSV)

2. **Script:**
   ```
   "Hi, I'm looking to send some information about energy cost
   reduction to your facilities director. Could you tell me their
   name and email address?"
   ```

3. **Alternative script:**
   ```
   "Hi, I need to send a proposal to your VP of Operations. What's
   the best email address to reach them at?"
   ```

4. **If they ask why:**
   ```
   "We provide AI-powered energy management for data centers and
   wanted to share some case studies showing 25-30% cost reductions."
   ```

5. **What usually happens:**
   - Small companies: Receptionist gives you the info
   - Large companies: "Please send to info@company.com and we'll forward"
   - Medium companies: "Let me transfer you" â†’ talk to facilities directly!
